Designing with

I'm Mohamed Mahmoud, a 23-year-old Graphic Designer and a 2024 graduate of the Faculty of Specific Education, Department of Technology Education, Minia University, with a Very Good grade.

As a Mid-Level Graphic Designer, I specialize in creating visually engaging content that combines creativity with marketing objectives. Throughout my career, I have worked in both freelance and in-house environments, collaborating remotely with local and international companies on branding, social media campaigns, and commercial design projects.

My goal is to create designs that not only look great but also communicate effectively and deliver measurable results.
